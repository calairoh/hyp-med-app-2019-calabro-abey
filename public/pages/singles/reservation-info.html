Reservation info

Welcome to our Festival website!
Here you can find all the events scheduled for this season, take a look on our performers, discover their biographies and the events they're going to discuss.

If you want to join us in one of our fantastic events, you have to reserve a place in order to make sure you follow the event in the best way.
To do this you have two options:

  • Reserve a place once you arrive to the event location
    In every event location you will find an our stand where you can buy a ticket.
  • Reserve a place on our website
    In order to make an online reservation, first of all you have to sign up on the website. After that, you will able to take a reservation simply go in an event page and click on Add reservation.
    Please note that you cannot do a reservation for a passed event and also that you can do a reservation till the booking is open. Reservations will be closed once there are no more seats available.

For any dupt or question, we invite to see our contact us page
